Texas counties, cities and school districts have more than $100 billion in tax-supported debt. Across Texas over the last decade, the total amount of outstanding local debt has grown faster than the state’s population. Use our Local Debt Explorer to find out how much tax-supported debt is held by your city, county and school district.

ABOUT

The series examines the factors driving local communities around Texas to issue billions of dollars in debt. The data comes from the Texas Bond Review Board and reflects each entity’s debt load as of August 2013.
